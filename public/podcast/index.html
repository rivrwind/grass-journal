Unpublished — a podcast about publishing a book I wrote.

A Body of Water is a book of poems I wrote over the last year. The writing follows a narrative arc through several watersheds and four years of time as I search for home and meaning in life. It is a story about staying true to myself.

Unpublished, this podcast, is about this book, but also about the creative process in general—the story of how this and other stories come to be. The Story of the Story.
