Laying on my back  
My back begins to warm  
The soft cushion of the bed  
I have no plans  
Only worries  

I remember an ivory bear  
With a turquoise arrowhead tied to it's back  
That I used to keep in a hidden wooden box  
Where I used to keep my worries
